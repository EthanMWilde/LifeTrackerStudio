VitaminTracker

Stay on top of your vitamins and nutrients.

VitaminTracker makes it easy to keep a real record of what you take and when — not just a reminder you dismiss, but a log you can actually look back on. Better yet, it all syncs to Apple Health.

Download from the App Store today

Screenshots

Features

  • Your entire vitamin cabinet Add every vitamin and supplement you take, with custom dosages, schedules, nutrient breakdowns per dose, and appearances.
  • Today's schedule See exactly what's due today, what you've taken, and what's still pending — all in one place at a glance.
  • Smart reminders Get nudged at the times you've chosen per vitamin — not a single generic daily alert.
  • Nutrient tracking Log nutrient intake across your whole stack and see your daily totals against targets and limits you set yourself.
  • Groups Bundle related vitamins into a group and log the whole group with one tap - great for when you take your morning doses.
  • Inventory tracking Track stock levels per vitamin. VitaminTracker deducts automatically as you log and notifies you when you're running low.
  • Streaks & reports See your consistency over weeks and months. Weekly and monthly reports show exactly how well you're keeping up with your stack.
  • CSV and PDF export Export your full history at any time to share with a practitioner or keep your own records.
  • Apple Health sync Logged doses write to Health automatically, so your supplement history sits alongside the rest of your health data.
  • Apple Watch app Browse your vitamins and log doses from your wrist.
  • Home Screen widgets Check off today's vitamins, view your schedule, or track a specific nutrient — all without opening the app.
  • Apple Shortcuts Log vitamins, groups, and water as part of automations or shortcuts triggered manually or via Siri.

VitaminTracker Pro

  • Unlimited vitamins The free plan tracks up to 3 vitamins. Pro removes the limit so you can stay on top of everything.
  • Custom nutrients & categories Create nutrients beyond the built-in library and organise them into your own categories.
  • Label scanner Point your camera at a supplement label to automatically fill in the nutrient fields when adding or editing a vitamin.
  • Tab bar customisation Choose which screens appear in your tab bar and in what order, so your most-used screens are always one tap away.
  • Custom app icons Choose from a range of alternative app icons to personalise VitaminTracker on your Home Screen.
