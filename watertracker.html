WaterTracker

Hydration, logged in one tap.

WaterTracker keeps hydration effortless — log a glass from your phone, your wrist, or your Home Screen, and watch your progress sync straight to Apple Health.

Download from the App Store today

Screenshots

Features

  • One-tap logging Custom presets mean most days you're just a single button to log your morning glass or bottle of water.
  • Timeline & reports Weekly and monthly bar charts, streaks, and records give you a clear view of your habits over time.
  • Apple Health sync Every log writes to Apple Health as a Dietary Water sample. WaterTracker can also read your Apple Health data from other apps.
  • Daily target & reminders Set a daily goal or use the built-in calculator for a personalised recommendation. Get reminders to drink throughout the day.
  • Apple Watch app Log water with ease from your wrist.
  • Home Screen widgets See your progress ring, log a preset, or view a weekly bar chart — all without opening the app.
